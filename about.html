About

Our Studio

Inclusive Yoga For Mind, Body, And Spirit

At Asha Yoga, we believe that yoga is for everyone. Our mission is to create an inclusive and welcoming environment where individuals from all walks of life can practice and grow. Through yoga, we foster inner peace, strength, and inspiration. Our diverse classes are designed to help you discover and embrace your full potential.

Community is at the heart of Asha Yoga. We strive to build a nurturing environment where everyone feels valued and connected. Join us and be a part of our inclusive, vibrant community.

Meet Hope

Studio Founder And Owner

Hope Haskins, RYT-500, E-RYT200, YACEP, IAYT, MA holder, lives on her family farm with her husband, two toddlers, and many animals. Her journey with yoga began as a way to reduce anxiety and find balance in life. Today, Hope shares her knowledge and passion through her classes, helping others find strength, stability, and inner peace.

Hope focuses on community building within rural areas and bringing yoga to all levels of experience. Her dedication to making yoga accessible to everyone drives her passion for teaching and supporting her students.

Our Teachers

Elena Georgiades

Elena began teaching PCP Pilates during the pandemic, enhancing her enthusiasm for group fitness and movement. As a classically trained musician, she discovered a passion for teaching during the lockdown. Elena's classes focus on mobility and core strength, providing a safe and supportive environment for all levels.

Sarah Davis

Sarah has been practicing yoga for seven years and began teaching to deepen her own practice. She focuses on creating a supportive and inclusive environment for all her students. Sarah's classes emphasize body alignment and breath control.

Stephany Vaughn-Mack

Stephany is dedicated to bringing fun and energy to her classes. With a background in dance and fitness, her yoga sessions combine dynamic movement and mindfulness. Stephany's classes are accessible to all levels and aim to provide a holistic yoga experience.
