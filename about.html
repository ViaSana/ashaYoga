Our Studio

Inclusive Yoga For Mind, Body, And Spirit

At Asha Yoga, we believe that yoga is for everyone. Our mission is to create an inclusive and welcoming environment where individuals from all walks of life can practice and grow. We provide a space for physical, mental, and spiritual well-being to flourish in harmony. We focus on the transformative power of yoga to foster inner peace, strength, and resilience. Our diverse classes cater to all levels, from seasoned yogis to beginners. Our experienced instructors offer personalized guidance and support to help you deepen your practice.

Community is at the heart of Asha Yoga. We strive to build a nurturing environment where everyone feels valued and connected. Our studio is not just a place to practice yoga but a sanctuary for solace, encouragement, and inspiration. Inclusivity is a core value at Asha Yoga. We celebrate diversity and ensure our studio is a safe space for all, regardless of age, ability, background, or identity. We are passionate about making yoga accessible to everyone.

Join us at Asha Yoga to experience the holistic benefits of yoga in a community that embraces you just as you are. Together, we cultivate a deeper sense of well-being and unity, both on and off the mat.

Meet Hope

Studio Founder And Owner

Hope Hawkins, RYT500, E-RYT200, YACEP, and MA holder, lives on her family farm with her husband, two toddlers, and many animals. Her journey into yoga began as a way to soothe anxiety and find direction in life. Through yoga, she discovered her true passion: helping others (and herself) grow in strength, stability, and clarity.

With over a decade of experience, Hope brings a deep passion for holistic wellness and a commitment to making yoga accessible to everyone. Her teaching style emphasizes inclusivity and personal growth, ensuring all students feel welcome and supported.

Hope focuses on community building within rural areas and bringing yoga to diverse populations. Introduced to Ashtanga in 2021, she practices this form personally and is excited to show that Ashtanga is an accessible and sustainable yoga practice.
